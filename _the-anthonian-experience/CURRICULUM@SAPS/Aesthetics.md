<p>At SAPS, Anthonians discover and develop their artistic voices through
immersive experiences in the arts. In Visual Arts, students embark on creative
journeys as they explore and experiment with diverse techniques, expressing
their unique perspectives and ideas. Through Music, Anthonians discover
the joy of creating, performing and appreciating music together, building
confidence in their abilities.</p>

<p>Our students forge meaningful connections between the arts and their daily
lives, finding creative inspiration in other subjects and their personal
experiences. Through our dynamic Art and Music curricula, Anthonians actively
engage in creative practice, developing critical thinking skills as they
explore various styles and forms of art. As they progress, students grow
into confident individuals who appreciate diverse forms of creative expression
and contribute their unique voices to our vibrant school community.</p>

<h4>Visual Arts</h4>
<p>Through our Art Curriculum, students:</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Discover and experiment with diverse art forms,
from traditional 2D and 3D art to contemporary digital art and ceramics,
expanding their creative possibilities</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Develop a discerning eye for art appreciation
as they explore works from different periods, cultures, and countries,
understanding how artists express their unique perspectives</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Cultivate their artistic vocabulary and critical
thinking skills by analysing artworks through elements like shape, form,
colour, lines, and texture, drawing connections between different artistic
styles</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Take pride in sharing their creative journeys
and artworks with peers, learning to articulate their artistic process
and decisions</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Transform inspiration from master artists into
their own interpretations using various art mediums</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Embrace technology through our Digital Art Programme
(SAPS LLP), exploring new frontiers of artistic creation using ICT tools</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Explore the versatility of clay through our
specialised Ceramics Programme (SAPS LLP), developing skills in shaping
and sculpting three-dimensional forms .</p>
