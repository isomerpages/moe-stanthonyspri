<h2>InfoComm Club</h2>
<p>The Infocomm Club was established in January 2007 and the Club focuses
on developing digital media skills in its members.</p>
<p>Objective(s):
<br>-&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To excite students about Information Communication
and Technology (ICT) in a fun and meaningful way by helping them to learn
various multimedia skills such as use of coding, animations and games development.
<br>-&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Aims to cultivate leadership and entrepreneurship
capabilities at an early age.
<br>-&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To create opportunities for students to
think creatively, reason systematically and work collaboratively.</p>
<p><strong><em>Activities</em>:</strong>
<br>-&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Members will learn to program their own
interactive stories, games, animations and codes.
<br>-&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Members will share their creation with
others in the online community.</p>
